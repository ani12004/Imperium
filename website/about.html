About Imperium

Imperium was built with a single mission: to provide the most comprehensive, reliable, and user-friendly Discord bot experience.

Our Mission

BleedX was created with a single goal: to provide the ultimate Discord experience. We believe that managing a server should be easy, fun, and engaging for everyone. Whether you're looking for advanced moderation tools, a global economy system, or just some fun social commands, BleedX has you covered.

The Technology

Built on the powerful Discord.js v14 library and Node.js, BleedX leverages the latest technologies to ensure speed, reliability, and security. Our infrastructure is designed to handle thousands of servers with minimal latency.
