Bot Commands

Explore the full capabilities of Discord Bot Universal.

Economy

s?balance
Check your wallet and bank balance.
s?deposit <amount>
Deposit money into your bank.
s?withdraw <amount>
Withdraw money from your bank.
s?pay <user> <amount>
Transfer money to another user.
s?work
Work to earn money.
s?daily
Claim your daily reward.
s?rob <user>
Attempt to rob a user.

Social & OwO

s?profile
View your profile, partner, and family.
s?setbio <text>
Set your profile biography.
s?marry <user>
Propose to a user.
s?divorce
Divorce your partner.
s?family
View your family tree.
s?quote <text>
Generate a quote image.
s?hug / kiss / slap
Interact with other users (GIFs).
s?hunt
Hunt for animals.
s?zoo
View your animal collection.

Moderation

s?ban <user>
Ban a member from the server.
s?kick <user>
Kick a member from the server.
s?timeout <user>
Timeout a member.
s?warn <user>
Warn a user.
s?nuke
Delete and recreate the channel.
s?lock / unlock
Lock or unlock the channel.

Tickets

s?tsetup
Setup the ticket panel.
s?tclose
Close the current ticket.
s?tadd <user>
Add a user to the ticket.

Utility

s?help
Show the help menu.
s?poll <question>
Create a simple yes/no poll.
s?userinfo <user>
View user information.
s?serverinfo
View server information.

Fun

s?joke / meme
Get a random joke or meme.
s?rps
Play Rock, Paper, Scissors.
s?cat / dog
Get a random animal picture.

Leveling

s?rank <user>
View rank card.
s?levels
View the leaderboard.
