Common Issues

Bot not responding?

Make sure the bot has the correct permissions (View Channel, Send Messages) in your channel. Check if the prefixes match.

Slash commands missing?

Re-invite the bot with the applications.commands scope enabled. It may take up to an hour for Discord to update.

Music not playing?

Ensure the bot is not deafened by the server and has permission to connect and speak in the voice channel.
